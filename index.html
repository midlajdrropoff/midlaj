SEO

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the practice of improving a website so it ranks higher in search engine results (like Google). It focuses on optimizing content, keywords, website structure, page speed, and backlinks to increase organic (non-paid) traffic.

SMM

SMM (Social Media Marketing) is the use of social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, X (Twitter), and LinkedIn to promote products, services, or brands. It involves creating and sharing content, running ads, and engaging with audiences to increase brand awareness, drive traffic, and boost sales.

SEM

SEM (Search Engine Marketing) is a digital marketing strategy used to increase a website’s visibility on search engine results pages through paid advertising. It mainly involves running paid search ads (such as Google Ads) using targeted keywords to drive immediate traffic and generate leads or sales.
